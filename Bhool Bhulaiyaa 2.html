Bhool Bhulaiyaa 2

Bhool Bhulaiyaa 2 (transl. Labyrinth 2) is a 2022 Indian Hindi-language comedy horror film directed by Anees Bazmee, written by Aakash Kaushik and Farhad Samji, and produced by Bhushan Kumar and Krishan Kumar under the banner T-Series Films and Murad Khetani and Anjum Khetani under the banner Cine1 Studios. A standalone sequel to Bhool Bhulaiyaa (2007), the film stars Tabu, Kartik Aaryan, and Kiara Advani. The plot follows Ruhaan Randhawa (Aaryan), who has to pretend to be a fraud psychic to deal with the return of Manjulika (Tabu), a malevolent spirit hell-bent on revenge against the Thakur family.
