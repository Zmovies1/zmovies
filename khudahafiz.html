Khuda Haafiz: Chapter 2 – Agni Pariksha (transl. May God be your protector: Chapter 2 – Trial by Fire) is a 2022 Indian Hindi-language action thriller film and a sequel to the 2020 film Khuda Haafiz. The film is written and directed by Faruk Kabir and produced by Kumar Mangat Pathak, Abhishek Pathak, Sneha Bimal Parekh and Ram Mirchandani under the banner of Panorama Studios. It stars Vidyut Jammwal and Shivaleeka Oberoi.[4][5] The film was released theatrically on 8 July 2022.[1]
